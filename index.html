Why this matters
You said it yourself: scrolling social media all day is your biggest productivity killer. Logging it daily makes the invisible visible.
The goal: Start at whatever is honest. Get it below 60 min/day in week 1. Below 30 min/day in week 2. Below 15 min/day in week 3. Every minute saved = one more outreach message you could send.
The rule: When you go over budget, don't beat yourself up. Just log it, see it, and do better tomorrow. Awareness is the first step.
Focus Playbook
Your personal rules for staying on track.
Non-negotiable rules
Phone rule: Delete all social media apps from your phone. You will gain back 2–3 hours every single day.
Morning rule: No phone for the first 30 minutes after waking. Just coffee.
Wolt rule: Fixed hours only — e.g. Mon/Wed/Fri 5–9pm. When the shift ends, close the app.
Priority rule: Every morning ask: "What is the ONE thing that would move my consultancy forward today?" Do that first.
Interns rule: One specific measurable task per week. "Find 20 contacts" not "help with BD."
Your client strategy
Stop targeting startups. Target Danish SMEs (50–200 employees) in logistics, manufacturing, retail, healthcare.
Your unfair advantage: You build real products (Smart Guide). You're not just a consultant — lead with that.
International clients: UK and US care less about where you're from. Try LinkedIn outreach, Toptal, Contra.
Danish partner: Show up at events. Talk about Smart Guide. The right person will be attracted to what you're building.
